Fishing for the Moon

Composite Photography

I've always thought of my drawings as a composite of ideas. I wanted to use one of them as the basis for a composite in Photoshop. A contrast between analog and digital art, the two pieces tell slightly different stories. While the original is a paper boat out at sea, this one portrays someone fishing for the moon.
