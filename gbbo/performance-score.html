What is the Performance Score?

Five things can happen to a baker during a week in the tent: be in the running for Star Baker, awarded the Star Baker crown, be comfortably in the middle of the pack, in danger of elimination, or get eliminated. The Performance Score is an indication of how well a baker performed during their run, however long, averaging their finishes week-to-week. The equation is shown below.

The Math

s = weeks as Star Baker
t = weeks in the running for Star Baker

n = weeks in the middle of the pack
d = weeks in danger of elimination

w = baker's total weeks in competition

Perfect Score: 10

The Performance Score Scale

This scale represents the categories that a baker can be sorted into, based on average finish of the five possible outcomes for each week. The distribution is imbalanced towards the low end, given contestants that spent little time in the tent have only negative performance to review. Thus, it makes a baker truly special if they can average even a good score, putting them in the 28th percentile of bakers overall.
