PRINTED POEMS

OVERVIEW

A friend of mine came to me saying his dad was trying to make some wall art to decorate their new house. He was using PowerPoint and (shockingly) wasn't that happy with the results. They hired me to create large poster size prints that would beautifully combine four poems with images of nature. Knowing that these prints wouldn't hang next to each other, I worked to make the designs cohesive, while also giving each one a unique identity.
