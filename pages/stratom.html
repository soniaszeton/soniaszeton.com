OVERVIEW

Stratom is an advanced technology company focused on developing autonomous systems that solve real-world challenges in defense and commercial applications. They came to us to redesign their website and create new branding collatoral for the company including business cards, a letterhead and a presentation template. The design of their website hadn't been updated since 2001, it was definitley in need of an update to say the least. After collaborating closely with the client, we were able to debut a new, modern website that ecapsulates the core of what Stratom is. We were delighted to hear that they were contacted by a company with an opportunity for a multi-million dollar contract solely based on the new website!
