Bee Kind

Branding | Logo | UI

Bee Kind is an imagined company that aims to address the rapid decrease of the honeybee population. Loosely using a UX design process as a guide, I started by researching the problem and any similar organizations that attempt to address the problem, then came up with a mission statement. From there I identified the audience and worked through some user stories and flows. After that I started working on the wireframes of the UI and narrowing in on the tone of the site. Finally I created a logo that felt true to the brand of the company and that would appeal to the target audience. Tying everything together from the research to the user flows, I created a high fidelity mockup of the website's home page.
