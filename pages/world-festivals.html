WORLD FESTIVALS

OVERVIEW

An animated infographic exploring 4 different fesivals from all around the world during each of the 4 seasons. The Cooper Hill Cheese Roll in England in the Spring, the Tomatina Festival in Spain in the Summer, the Day of the Skulls in Bolivia in Autumn, and the Harbin Ice Festival in China in the Winter. All the graphics were created using Illustrator and the animation was done with After Effects.
