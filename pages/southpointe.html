OVERVIEW

Village Capital came to our company with the goal of raising public awareness about a new housing development called Southpointe @ 108. To do this we needed to create a logo and brand identity along with an informational website all within an extremely tight deadline. After defining a color pallete and designing a logo the client was happy with, I got to work on the website. This is a copy heavy site, there was a lot of information to convey and some concerns to address about the new development. To break up the long scroll I created a series of custom icons and other visual elements to compliment the copy. To help with the awareness campaign I developed social media graphics and designed presentation decks for various City Council and neighborhood meetings.
