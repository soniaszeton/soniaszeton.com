Phoenix

Tangible Typography

This piece transforms the word "Redemption" from an abstract idea to something physical. The phoenix has always been tied to the idea of rebirth and redemption. I set out to create a phoenix out of matches with the word Redemption within it. After setting it alight, I edited the video so the phoenix would emerge brand new from the flames.
