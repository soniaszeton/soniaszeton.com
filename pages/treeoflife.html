Tree of Life

Hardware & IoT | Fabrication

Tree of Life is a responsive art installation that displays the current emotional tone of Twitter by keeping track of certain keywords associated with different emotions and changing the lights on the tree accordingly. Each color on the tree represents a different emotion, red for anger, yellow for happiness, etc. I used a Twitter API to pull all of the tweets containing the chosen keywords every 10 seconds and then count how many of those tweets are angry or sad or happy, etc. Once all the tweets are counted the tree is lit proportionally, so if 35% of the tweets that were read in were happy then 35% of the lights on the tree will be yellow, and so on for the rest of the emotions.
